# Questions to Help Shape a Writing Topic


1. What’s at stake?
2. How much background information will be needed?
3. Is a definition needed?
4. Is there common ground?
5. Can any concessions be made?
6. What are the advantages to accepting the proposition?
7. What are the disadvantages to accepting the proposition?
8. Which method (Block or Alternating) will be more suitable?
9. What’s the best way to conclude this argument?
